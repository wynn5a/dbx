Focus

Focus is always a 2px accent outline (1px offset for buttons), never a glow. Selected grid cells use an inset accent-line ring instead of an outline.

Disabled

Disablement drops opacity to 40% for filled controls; inputs fade to quaternary text on the panel surface with a soft hairline. Cursor goes not-allowed.
