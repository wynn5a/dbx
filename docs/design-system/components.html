Components

The working vocabulary. Borderless surfaces, hairline dividers, and one accent for primary actions and focus. Hover lifts the background by a hair; active state fills it slightly more. Icons are 1.5px line icons sized 11–17px.

Buttons

One hierarchy, four weights. Primary is the lone accent action per view; secondary is a filled neutral for the next-most-likely action; outline is the everyday hairline chip; ghost is borderless for low-stakes or in-toolbar actions. All share 6px radius, 12.5px medium text, and a 120ms ease.
